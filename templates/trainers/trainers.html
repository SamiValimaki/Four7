Jason Mertlich - Head Coach
Coach Jason Mertlich is quickly becoming one of the premier instructors of elite fighters in the world. Over the years, he has taken the best, most combat specific aspects of Jiu-Jitsu, Wrestling, Boxing, Muay Thai and numerous other martial arts to create FOUR7 Jiu-Jitsu. In just a few short years of coaching his resume is packed full of UFC standouts! FOUR7 Jiu-Jitsu has its roots in the virtues of the Bushido: Rectitude, Benevolence, Respect, Courage, Honesty, Honor, and Loyalty
